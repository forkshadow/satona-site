FAQ

Des réponses simples.

Is SATONA an electronic wallet?

No. SATONA is presented as a physical medium for recording BIP39 recovery-word information in binary.

Should I enter my complete phrase here?

No. Use the online tools only for learning or a single-word conversion. Prefer the downloadable list for an offline workflow.

Are wallet logos endorsements?

No. They indicate BIP39 compatibility context only and do not imply a commercial relationship.
