# SATONA V2 — content requiring confirmation

Do not publish the following as facts until supplied and verified by SATONA.

1. **Price and currency** — final price, VAT treatment, promotions and checkout wording.
2. **Exact kit contents** — every item included in the box, including any punch or accessory.
3. **Materials and dimensions** — plate alloy/grade, thickness, width, height, weight and finish.
4. **Durability evidence** — test methods, conditions and substantiated fire/water/corrosion/impact claims.
5. **Delivery** — countries served, costs, carrier, dispatch time and tracking process.
6. **Returns and warranty** — eligibility, period, exclusions, procedure and legal basis.
7. **Company contact and legal identity** — business name, address, email and support hours.
8. **Legal notices and privacy policy** — jurisdiction-appropriate pages and cookie requirements.
9. **Stock and availability** — only if live inventory information is available.
10. **Accessibility/contact escalation** — an accessible support route for purchase or product questions.
