Security principles

Das Geheimnis schützen, nicht nur das Objekt.

SATONA ist ein physisches Backup-Medium für eine BIP39-Wiederherstellungsphrase. Gute Sicherheit hängt weiterhin von Erstellung, Prüfung und Aufbewahrung ab.

Widerstandsillustration

What SATONA can help with

It provides a physical format for recording BIP39-word information in a binary layout, independent from a connected device.

What it cannot protect

It cannot prevent theft, loss, coercion, errors in transcription or unsafe storage. No physical medium removes the need for a security plan.

Good practice

Keep recovery data out of connected environments.

1. Create carefully

Never expose a complete recovery phrase to a website, cloud service or camera.

2. Verify manually

Compare each word and its 11-bit pattern before final storage.

3. Store deliberately

Choose a location appropriate to your own risks, access needs and privacy requirements.

Use BIP39 compatibility with care.

Compatibility describes a recovery-phrase standard; it is not a guarantee of recovery or a substitute for verifying your own workflow.
